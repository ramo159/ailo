About Ailo

Ailo is a privacy minded AI guide designed to keep you focused while you ask questions, capture quick ideas, or continue research. The experience pairs immersive gradients and glass UI treatments with a simple workflow: open the chat, ask your question, and stay in flow.

Key highlights include:

  • Instant access — no login, no saved transcripts.
  • Fullscreen mode that minimizes distractions and highlights your conversation.
  • Export ready transcripts for sharing summaries or follow ups.
  • Responsive layout tuned for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
